===========================================
SECKINGER JAGUARS APP - DEPLOYMENT GUIDE
===========================================

OPTION 1: NETLIFY (Easiest - Free, 2 minutes)
----------------------------------------------
1. Go to https://netlify.com and sign up free
2. Drag and drop this entire folder onto the Netlify dashboard
3. You get a link like: https://seckinger-jaguars.netlify.app
4. Share that link with parents & players!

TO ADD TO YOUR IPHONE HOME SCREEN:
1. Open the link in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add" — done! It'll appear as an app icon.

TO ADD TO ANDROID HOME SCREEN:
1. Open the link in Chrome
2. Tap the 3-dot menu
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add" — done!

OPTION 2: GITHUB PAGES (Also free)
-----------------------------------
1. Create a GitHub account at github.com
2. Create a new repository called "seckinger-jaguars"
3. Upload all files in this folder
4. Go to Settings > Pages > Deploy from branch (main)
5. Your site will be at: https://[yourusername].github.io/seckinger-jaguars
